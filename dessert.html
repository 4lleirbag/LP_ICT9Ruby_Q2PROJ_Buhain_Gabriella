Banana Split INGREDIENTS

Vanilla Ice Cream, Chocolate Ice Cream, Strawberry Ice Cream, Banana, Whipped Cream, Strawberry Syrup, Cherries(Optional)

 STEPS Split the banana in half vertically and place them on the sides.
Get one scoop of each ice cream and align them between the sliced bananas.
Use the whipped cream on top of the ice cream (and use the strawberry syrup on top).
Add the cherry on top of the whipped cream.
Leche Flan INGREDIENTS

1 tbsp of sugar per Ilanera, 10 large egg yolks, 12 oz Condensed Milk, 14 oz evaporated milk, 1/4 tsp salt, 1/2 tbsp lemon/lime juice, 1/2 tsp vanilla extract.

 STEPS

Heat up the steamer to prepare it.
Add Ilanera and caramelize the sugar on an open flame. (No burning of sugar).
In a bowl, speperate the yolks from the egg whites. Add the egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, salt, lime juice, and vanilla extract in a bowl and mix until one colour. (Do not overmix).
Pour leche flan mixture into cooled Ilanera and cover with aluminum foil.
When the water is boiling, put the Ilanera to the steamer and wait for 35-45 mins.
Pull out the Ilanera. Poke with a toothpick to know if cooked. If toothpick is clean, it is cooked.
Wait for it to cool down or leave it in the refrigerator overnight.

Yogurt Parfait INGREDIENTS

1 cup mixed berries fresh or frozen, 1/2 cup fat-free yogurt, any flavour, 1/4 cup granola/high protein breakfast cereal

 STEPS

Layer half of the fruit, yogurt and granola/cereal in a small glass
Make another layer, ending with cereal and berries on top.
Serve immediately.
